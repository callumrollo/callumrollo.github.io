Teaching

ERDDAP

I have taught various in person and online seminars on the ERDDAP data broker. The materials are all available on github: - 2023 January, Gothenburg repo - 2023 Autumn, GOOS online repo. Recordings to be added

Python

I have taught several courses in Python at UEA. Most recently the 2020 Cefas/Aries Introduction to Python. This course is taught in cooperation with 2 instructors from Cefas and two from UEA. The course was originally developed by Denis Sergeev and has evolved over the last 4 iterations. This is a comprehensive 3 day course.

I have written my own, shorter Python courses. These are taught in three half day sessions at UEA for post graduate researchers and staff. The materials are available here and are designed for self teaching as much as possible. Model solutions to problems are included.

I have given various code tutorials to the UEA Scientific Python group.

During the 2022 ITGC cruise to Thwaites glacier, I taught introductory courses in Python and git.

The Carpentries

As of April 2021 I am a Carpentries certified instructor. I taught my first course at NOAA in May 2021.
